Instagram layout SDF 06

An Instagram layout

For this project what I did was using the given information I had to try and recreate the Instagram profile section.

I started by making the theme dark and realigning the top part of the page to replicate Instagram. I then just changed some sizes and orders around to better fit the page.

I then aligned the images and made them dynamicly adjust the rows and columns as the screen size changed. After that I added some hover effects on the images and added a hover effect on the settings and edit buttons.
